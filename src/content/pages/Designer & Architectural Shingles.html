Designer & Architectural Shingles

Enhance your home’s curb appeal while strengthening its protection with premium designer and architectural shingles. These high-quality shingles offer a dimensional, high-end appearance that mimics the look of natural slate or wood shake while delivering superior durability compared to traditional 3-tab shingles. Our team installs every roof with precision to ensure long-lasting performance and a clean, polished finish.

Designer and architectural shingles are a great option for homeowners who want both style and strength, giving your roof a distinctive look that stands out while protecting your home from Oklahoma’s demanding weather.

Key Features:

  • Dimensional shingles that create a rich, layered appearance
  • Greater durability and lifespan than standard 3-tab shingles
  • Wide selection of colors and styles to match your home’s design

Process

A custom process just for you!

  • 01

    Consultation & Style Selection

    We begin by discussing the look you want for your home and reviewing the available designer and architectural shingle options. Our team helps you select colors and styles that best complement your home’s exterior.

  • 02

    Roof Preparation

    Before installation, we carefully remove old roofing materials and inspect the roof decking to ensure a solid foundation. Any necessary repairs are addressed to prepare the roof for long-term performance.

  • 03

    Expert Shingle Installation

    Our roofing professionals install your designer or architectural shingles with precision, ensuring proper layering, ventilation, and flashing for maximum durability and weather resistance.

  • 04

    Final Walkthrough & Quality Check

    After installation is complete, we conduct a detailed inspection and walk you through the finished roof. This final step ensures everything meets our quality standards and that you’re fully satisfied with the result.
