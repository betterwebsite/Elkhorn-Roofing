Who We Are Elkhorn Roofing

Elkhorn Roofing was born out of a simple belief: homeowners deserve honest answers and quality work they can trust. After seeing too many rushed jobs, cut corners, and confusing insurance conversations, I decided to start a roofing company that did things differently-one that focused on education, clear communication, and long‑term protection instead of quick sales.

I’m passionate about helping families feel confident about the roof over their heads. That means taking time to explain options, showing photos of what we find, and recommending what’s truly best for each home-not just what’s easiest to sell. I got started in the industry as a sales consoltant in the field, learning how roofs are built from the deck up, and I’ve carried that experience into every project we take on today.

Yes, we have a lot of experience-not only installing roofs, but dealing with Oklahoma’s storms, hail, custom homes, and insurance claims. That experience is what allows us to specialize in Class 3 and Class 4 impact‑resistant shingles and to stand behind our workmanship with confidence. Elkhorn Roofing exists to be the roofing partner you can rely on year after year, not just for one job. Our expeience in our roofs comes from building over 400 plus custom homes over the past 30 years.
