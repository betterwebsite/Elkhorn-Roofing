Process

A custom process just for you!

  • 01

    Consultation & Material Selection

    We start by discussing your goals for your home’s exterior and helping you choose the best siding materials, colors, and styles to match your home’s design and improve curb appeal.

  • 02

    Preparation & Surface Inspection

    Our team carefully prepares the exterior of your home by removing old siding if needed and inspecting the underlying structure to ensure a solid, weather-resistant foundation for the new siding.

  • 03

    Professional Siding Installation

    Using precise installation techniques, our experienced team installs your new siding to ensure proper alignment, secure fastening, and long-term durability against wind, rain, and Oklahoma’s changing weather.

  • 04

    Final Inspection & Clean Finish

    Once the installation is complete, we perform a detailed quality check and clean the work area. We walk you through the finished result to ensure everything meets our standards and your expectations.
